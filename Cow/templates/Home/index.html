About KAMADHENU
GAUSHALA

Kamadhenu gausala was started in 2008.Beginning with only 10 cattle the number of today is icreased to 170. One of the unique features at our gaushala is the personal care given to the indivisual cattle. Pregnant cows are offered special diet and care to ensure proper health of the Cow and the calf. Old cows and bulls are given special care.The health status of each and every cow is noted on a daily basis and is logged for long term monitoring. Apart from providing clean hygienic surroundings, fresh water and ventilation, great care is taken with regards to their diet.
